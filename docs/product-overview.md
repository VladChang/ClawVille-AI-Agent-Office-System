# Product Overview

ClawVille is a dashboard that presents the behaviour of a multi‑agent OpenClaw deployment as though it were a team of workers in an office.  At its core it provides a unified interface for monitoring, controlling and analysing agent‑based workflows.

The product has two primary audiences:

* **Operations engineers** who need to understand system state quickly and respond to errors.  They benefit from summary counts, lists of agents and tasks, logs and control operations such as pause or retry.  
* **General users or stakeholders** who appreciate an intuitive, humanised view of the agents.  The office map and thought bubbles make it clear what the AI is doing without sifting through raw logs.

Key benefits of ClawVille include:

1. **Observability** – Real‑time visibility of agent status, tasks, dependencies and recent events.
2. **Control** – Ability to pause, resume or reassign agents and tasks directly from the console.
3. **Insight** – Derived metrics and analytics highlight hotspots, bottlenecks and failure patterns.
4. **Humanisation** – An optional office view that makes the system approachable for non‑engineers.

ClawVille can be deployed alongside any OpenClaw system that exposes the required agent and task events.  It is designed to be extended with custom analytics or visualisation modules.
